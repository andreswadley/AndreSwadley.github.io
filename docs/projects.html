On Meaning: A Dillema

    Intention can not be held in the mind//
In an effort to gain superiority these two objects only end up hurting the thing they claim to be helping// Unsatisfied with its position the garden axe further tries leveraging itself over the bird// In the midst of making claims about its use the knife abuses its power to wound//
    The objects have no emotional objectives associated and therefore do not have intention//
The objects have no intention therefore they do not care about repercussions//
